# Vigilant Futures Journal - Setup Guide

Thank you for purchasing the **Vigilant Futures Journal**. This professional-grade trading workstation is designed for high-performance futures traders.

## 🚀 5-Minute Setup
1. **Unzip** the files on your computer.
2. **Setup Firebase:** Go to [console.firebase.google.com](https://console.firebase.google.com/), create a free project, and enable "Google Auth" and "Firestore".
3. **Connect your App:** Replace the contents of `firebase-applet-config.json` with your project's web configuration details.
4. **Deploy:** Use any free hosting like Vercel, Netlify, or Firebase Hosting to put your link live!

## 🛡️ Privacy First
Your data is stored in **your** Google Cloud account. No one else has access to your trades, mistakes, or psychological logs.
